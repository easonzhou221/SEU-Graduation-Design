# SEU-Graduation-Design

**Research on Voice Activity Detection under Low SNR Conditions：**
1.Study the fundamental concepts of acoustic signal processing, understand the basic mechanisms of speech production, and explore feature representation methods for speech signals.
2.Master the basic principles of statistical signal detection, understand and implement typical Voice Activity Detection (VAD) methods, and analyze their respective characteristics. Discuss the primary factors influencing speech detection and analyze the impact of various simulation parameters on detection performance.
3.Study the basic principles and architectures of deep neural networks; design and implement a deep-learning-based speech signal detector, followed by a comprehensive analysis of its detection performance.

**低信噪比条件下的语音信号检测方法研究:**
1.学习声学信号处理的基本概念，了解语音信号产生的基本机理，了解语音信号的特征表征方法。
2.掌握统计信号检测的基本原理，理解并掌握典型的语音信号检测方法并分析这些方法的特点，讨论影响语音信号检测的主要因素，分析仿真检测方法中的各参数对于检测性能的影响。
3.学习深度网络的基本工作原理，了解深度网络的基本结构，设计并实现一种基于深度网络的语音信号检测器，并分析网络的检测性能。
